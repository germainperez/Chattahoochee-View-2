Buford Dam Landing

The river here can be run year-round but releases from Buford Dam can create unsafe conditions. Boaters and anglers should use extreme caution when traveling this section.

Atop the grassy knoll above the dam's powerhouse, you may see several goats. Since the 1980s, The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers has maintained a small herd to keep vegetation atop the dam trimmed.
